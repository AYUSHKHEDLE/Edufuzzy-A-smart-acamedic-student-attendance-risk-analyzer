### 🎓 Edufuzzy: Smart Academic Attendance Risk Analyzer

**Edufuzzy** is an intelligent analytical tool that uses **Fuzzy Logic** to move beyond binary "present/absent" tracking. It evaluates nuanced student attendance patterns to identify academic risk levels before they become critical.

---

## 🚀 Overview
Traditional systems only flag students once they fail. **Edufuzzy** changes the narrative from reactive to proactive by identifying the "sliding scale" of absenteeism—categorizing students into risk tiers (Low, Moderate, High, Critical) using soft computing.

## ✨ Key Features
* **Fuzzy Logic Engine:** Categorizes risk based on consistency and trends, not just rigid raw percentages.
* **Trend Detection:** Automatically identifies sudden shifts in student attendance behavior.
* **Predictive Insights:** Provides educators with actionable data for early intervention and counseling.
* **Interactive Dashboard:** Visualizes risk heatmaps and individual student trajectories for quick review.
